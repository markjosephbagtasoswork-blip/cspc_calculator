Predictive Logic

To accurately predict your grade mid-semester, any category without inputted scores automatically defaults to a perfect 100%. As you input your actual scores and deductions, the estimated grade drops to reflect your real maximum possible standing based on the CSPC Zero-Based formula.

About Trespassing

Trespassing is an unofficial, modern grade calculator built specifically for the students of Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges (CSPC). It operates entirely in your browser—no data is sent to external servers.

Grading System & Computation

The system maps percentages to the 1.00 - 5.00 scale based on the CSPC handbook. Passing grades range from 1.00 (99%) to 3.00 (75%).

GWA = Σ(Grade × Units) / Σ(Units)

CSPC adopts a zero-based grading formula for activities: (Raw Score ÷ Total Items) × 100.

Academic Honors Criteria

  • President's & Dean's Lister: You are automatically disqualified if you receive any grade lower than a 2.0 (e.g., 2.1, 2.5, 3.0, 5.0) in any enrolled course for that specific semester.
  • Latin Honors: To qualify for Summa, Magna, or Cum Laude, the only requirement is to reach the qualifying CGWA with no failing grade (5.0) on any course from your first year up to the current year.

Note: Grades in Pathfit are included in the GWA computation, while NSTP is excluded.
