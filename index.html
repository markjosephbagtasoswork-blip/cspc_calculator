About this Calculator

This calculator aims to help CSPC students efficiently calculate and track their General Weighted Average (GWA), honor standings, and estimate their course grades based on the Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges grading system. User inputs are not stored in any database. Everything is stored locally on your device.

Grading System & Course Work

The work of students shall be rated at the end of each semester/term in accordance with the CSPC grading system. The passing grades of the students range from 1.00 to 3.00, and the failing grade is 5.00.

Semestral GWA Computation

Semestral GWA is computed by multiplying the grade obtained in each subject by the corresponding units, getting the sum of these products, and dividing it by the total number of units for the semester.

GWA = Σ(Grade × Units) / Σ(Units)

Qualitative grades, HK/PE, NSTP, and Residency are not computed in the GWA. For SP/Thesis, when you receive a passing grade, it reflects in the entire units of the course.

Note: The regular load to qualify for honors is normally 15 units. An approved underload permit is required to remain eligible for academic honors if your load falls below this.

Cumulative Grade Computation

Cumulative GWA follows the same formula but uses all credited units across all valid semesters inputted into the cumulative tracker.

Course Grade Estimator Computation

CSPC adopts a zero-based grading formula computed as: (Raw Score ÷ Total Items) × 100.

  • Midterm Grade = Class Standing + Major Exam + Project
  • Tentative Final Grade = Class Standing + Major Exam + Project
  • Final Grade = (Midterm Grade + Tentative Final Grade) ÷ 2
